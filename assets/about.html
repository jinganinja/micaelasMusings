Micaela's Musings

Hello,

My name is Micaela. I am a 24 year old software developer on Vancouver Island, Canada. That’s one way to define myself anyway. I also go by dog Momma to my little rescue pup, Scotia, sibling to 6 earthly humans and 1 angel, and daughter of some pretty incredible parents. I like to spend time outside with the people and furry friends I care about most. I feel very drawn to the ocean and I adore the water.

My inspiration for this page stems from the loss of my little sister, Marisa, in December of 2019. I have often considered creating a personal webpage of sorts; however, aside from having a convenient display of some of my technical abilities, I didn’t have a lot of reason for one and we all know how that goes. After Marisa’s passing, I realized how strongly I felt about having some way to remember everything. Writing it down was alright, but I wanted to share it. Thus the birth of this page. Since conception, I have had many ideas of how I would like to grow this page into something more, but for now it’s pretty simple.

I have created this page completely on my own and currently it is just hosted on github. Maybe one day I will try something new, but for now this is a very easy way to host it! If you have any good ideas on how to host a site on my own I would love to hear about it.

Anyway, that’s a lot of chatter about me… But I suppose you did sign up for that when you clicked on this page. You can find more about me and what goes on inside my brain as you explore through this website I created for people like you!

Micaela (she/her)
